What I Do

It all started with an internship where I learned the ropes of web design using CMS tools like WordPress — and out of curiosity, I peeked behind the scenes and got hooked on PHP. Since then, I’ve gone on to develop websites and numerous mobile applications throughout my career. Now, I focus on delivering functional, user-friendly, and — yes — fun websites that not only work well but also bring ideas to life in a creative way.
